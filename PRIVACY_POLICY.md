# Privacy Policy

**Free Scanner**  
Last updated: May 25, 2026

## Overview

Free Scanner ("the App") is a document scanning application for Android developed by Ujjawal. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

## Information We Collect

### Camera & Storage Access
- The App accesses your device camera solely to capture document images.
- Images are processed locally on your device. We do not upload or store your scanned documents on any server.
- Exported files (PDF/JPEG) are saved to your device's local storage.

### Analytics Data
We use Google Firebase Analytics to collect anonymous usage data including:
- App opens and screen views
- Feature usage (scan count, filter usage, export format preference)
- Device type, OS version, and app version
- Crash reports

This data is anonymized and used solely to improve the App.

### Advertising
We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect:
- Device advertising ID
- General location (country/region level)
- App usage data for ad personalization

You can opt out of personalized ads in your device settings:  
**Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization**

## Data We Do NOT Collect
- We do not collect your name, email, or any personal contact information.
- We do not access, upload, or store your scanned documents.
- We do not collect precise GPS location.
- We do not sell any data to third parties.

## Third-Party Services

| Service | Purpose | Privacy Policy |
|---------|---------|---------------|
| Google Firebase Analytics | Usage analytics | [Firebase Privacy](https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy) |
| Google AdMob | Advertising | [Google Ads Privacy](https://policies.google.com/privacy) |
| Google ML Kit | OCR text extraction (on-device) | [ML Kit Terms](https://developers.google.com/ml-kit/terms) |

## Data Retention
Analytics data is retained for 14 months as per Google Analytics defaults. No personal data is stored on our servers.

## Children's Privacy
The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

## Changes to This Policy
We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted in the App or on this page.
